Vietnam Youth Economics Summer Forum

About Us

Economic forums are important events where socio-economic fluctuations are discussed and analyzed from the in-depth perspective of leading experts. For the first time, high school students nationwide will have the opportunity to participate in a fully simulated and practical version of the economic discussion format through the Vietnam Youth Economics Summer Forum.

Demographic

Students in Hanoi (Grades 9-12)

Interested or experienced in economics and finance

Enjoys engaging in forum-style discussions and debates

2025 Theme

The New Era (Kỷ Nguyên Vươn Mình)

This year's forum explores Vietnam's bold steps into a new economic era — one defined by innovation, resilience, and global engagement. “The New Era” challenges students to think critically about how youth can shape the future of economics in a rapidly changing world.

Topics

Vietnam and the Tariff Age: Adapting to Global Protectionism and Shifting Trade Policies

Beyond Flat Tax: Vietnam’s Push for Tax Transparency in Micro and Small Enterprises

The End of Cash? Vietnam’s Path Toward Digital Currencies and Financial Transformation
