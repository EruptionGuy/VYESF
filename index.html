About Us

Welcome to eCON Summer Camp! We're a team of enthusiasts on a mission to make economics fun and accessible. Through games, workshops, and real-world challenges, we turn big ideas into exciting adventures that inspire every camper to explore, think critically, and grow.

Main Activities

Econ Forum

A live, interactive conference where campers will take on the role of young economists, discussing and debating current global and local economic topics. (Topic to be finalized with the team.)

Company Tour

A field trip to Vietnam Finance, a leading financial company, to gain firsthand insight into the inner workings of the financial sector. Campers will learn how economic principles are applied in real business decisions.

Eligibility

Interested in economics, business, or how the world works financially

Curious, open-minded, and eager to learn through real-life experiences

Enjoys working in teams and participating in hands-on activities
