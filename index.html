Economic forums are important events where socio-economic fluctuations are discussed and analyzed from the in-depth perspective of leading experts. For the first time, Vietnamese high school students will have the opportunity to engage in a practical simulation of an economic summit, where pressing socio-economic issues are examined through different expert lenses at the Vietnam Youth Economics Summer Forum, held in-person in Hanoi.

Registration Deadline: 23:59PM 23/7/2025

Conference Date: 10/8/2025 (Full day)

2025 Theme

The New Era (Kỷ Nguyên Vươn Mình)

As Vietnam enters a period of rapid transformation, its economy is reshaping itself through digital innovation, shifting global partnerships, and a rising wave of youth-driven ambition. Under the theme “The New Era — Kỷ Nguyên Vươn Mình,” this year's forum invites students to explore how our nation is adapting, evolving, and stepping forward on the global stage.

There will be three main sessions. Each session will last three hours, all held in one day, with an estimated 25-40 delegates per session, divided into various sectoral groups depending on the topic (Delegates can express their preference of topic and sectoral group in the registration form).

Delegates are required to participate in at least two sessions, and may join up to three.

Panel Topics

Vietnam and the Tariff Age: Adapting to Global Protectionism and Shifting Trade Policies

Beyond Flat Tax: Vietnam's Push for Tax Transparency in Micro and Small Enterprises

The End of Cash? Vietnam's Path Toward Digital Currencies and Financial Transformation

Delegates will be provided with a conference e-guidebook after successful registration for clearer format instructions and content guidance.
